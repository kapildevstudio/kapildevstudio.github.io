Account & Data Deletion Request

How to delete your account and data: Send an email to studio.kapildev@gmail.com with subject line "Delete My Account — SNAKE Egg Hunter". We will process your request within 7 business days.

What Data Is Deleted

When you request account deletion, the following data is removed:

  • Your Google Sign-In association with our app
  • Your "Remove Ads" purchase status stored locally on your device
  • Any locally stored preferences (name, game settings)
Note: Since we do not operate backend servers, all your data is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app from your device will automatically delete all locally stored data immediately.

How to Delete Data from Your Device

  • Go to your phone Settings → Apps → SNAKE Egg Hunter
  • Tap "Clear Data" to remove all local data instantly
  • Or simply uninstall the app to remove everything

Google Sign-In Data

To revoke our app's access to your Google account:

  • Go to myaccount.google.com/permissions
  • Find SNAKE - Egg Hunter in the list
  • Click Remove Access
