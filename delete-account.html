Account and data deletion for SNAKE - Egg Hunter

If you want to remove your app-related data or revoke your connection to the app, this page explains the available options.

To request help directly, email studio.kapildev@gmail.com with the subject Delete My Account - SNAKE Egg Hunter. Requests are typically processed within 7 business days.

What can be deleted

  • Your Google Sign-In association with the app
  • Locally stored preferences such as guest name or settings
  • Local purchase-state information related to Remove Ads
Kapil Dev Studio does not operate a backend server for this app. Most app-related data is stored locally on your device, which means uninstalling the app or clearing its data removes that local information immediately.

How to delete local data from your device

  • Open your phone settings
  • Go to Apps and select SNAKE - Egg Hunter
  • Use Clear Data to remove local app data
  • Or uninstall the app to remove the stored data from your device

How to revoke Google account access

  • Visit myaccount.google.com/permissions
  • Find SNAKE - Egg Hunter in your connected apps list
  • Select Remove Access

Need help?

If you are unsure which deletion method is right for your situation, contact studio.kapildev@gmail.com.
