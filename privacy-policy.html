Privacy Policy

SNAKE - Egg Hunter

This Privacy Policy explains how Kapil Dev Studio collects, uses, and handles information when you use the SNAKE - Egg Hunter mobile application.

Last updated: March 14, 2026

By using the app, you agree to the practices described on this page. We aim to keep the experience simple and transparent, and we keep personal data collection limited to what is necessary for the app to function.

1. Information we collect

  • Google account information: If you sign in with Google, the app may access your name, email address, and profile image provided by Google Sign-In.
  • Guest name: If you use guest mode, the name you enter is stored locally on your device only.
  • Purchase information: If you purchase Remove Ads, payment processing is handled by Google Play Billing. We do not store your payment details.
  • Advertising data: The app uses Google AdMob, which may collect device identifiers and usage-related information to serve ads.

2. How information is used

  • To show your name or profile inside the app
  • To remember your Remove Ads purchase status
  • To display ads through Google AdMob
  • To improve app performance and user experience

3. Data storage

User preferences, guest name, and purchase status are stored locally on your device. Kapil Dev Studio does not operate a backend server for this app and does not store personal app data on its own servers.

4. Third-party services

The app uses third-party services that have their own privacy terms and policies.

  • Google Privacy Policy for Google AdMob and Google Sign-In
  • Google Play Terms for billing and purchase handling

5. Advertising and opt-out

Google AdMob may use the advertising ID on your device to serve personalized ads. You can control ad personalization through your device settings. Users can also remove ads in the app by purchasing the Remove Ads option when available.

6. Children's privacy

SNAKE - Egg Hunter is designed to be broadly accessible. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 beyond what may be provided through third-party services used in the app.

7. Data sharing

Kapil Dev Studio does not sell, rent, or trade personal information. Information may be handled by the third-party services listed above only for the functions they provide.

8. Data retention

Data stored locally on your device can be removed by clearing app data or uninstalling the app. Because we do not keep this data on our own servers, there is no separate remote data store maintained by Kapil Dev Studio for the app.

9. Security

We take reasonable care to keep the app experience secure. Third-party service providers such as Google are responsible for securing the data they process through their own systems and infrastructure.

10. Policy changes

This page may be updated from time to time. When changes are made, the Last updated date at the top of the page will be revised.

11. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact Kapil Dev Studio at studio.kapildev@gmail.com.
