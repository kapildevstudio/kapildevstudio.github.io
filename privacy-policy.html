Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Kapil Dev Studio ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the SNAKE — Egg Hunter mobile application ("the App"). By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Google Account Information (if you sign in with Google): Your name, email address, and profile picture, provided by Google Sign-In. This is used only to personalise your in-app experience (e.g. displaying your name).
  • Guest Name: If you play as a guest, the name you enter is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to any server.
  • Purchase Information: If you purchase "Remove Ads" (₹99), the transaction is processed entirely by Google Play Billing. We do not collect or store your payment details.
  • Advertising Data: Our app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve personalised ads. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To display your name and profile within the App
  • To remember your "Remove Ads" purchase status linked to your Google account
  • To serve advertisements via Google AdMob
  • To improve the App's performance and user experience

3. Data Storage

User preferences, name, and purchase status are stored locally on your device using browser localStorage within the App's WebView. No personal data is transmitted to or stored on our servers. We do not operate any backend servers.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services which have their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob — for displaying advertisements.
    Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Sign-In — for authentication.
    Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Billing — for in-app purchases.

5. Advertising and Opt-Out

We use Google AdMob to show banner and interstitial advertisements. AdMob may use your device's advertising ID to serve personalised ads. You can opt out of personalised advertising at any time by:

  • Going to your device Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation
  • Or by purchasing Remove Ads (₹99) within the App to remove all advertisements entirely

6. Children's Privacy

SNAKE — Egg Hunter is rated Everyone (E) and is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App does not require account creation to play — the Guest mode allows anyone to play without providing any personal information.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to any third parties. Information may be shared only with third-party services listed in Section 4 above, strictly for the purposes described.

8. Data Retention

All data stored locally on your device (name, preferences, purchase status) can be cleared at any time by uninstalling the App. Since we do not store data on external servers, there is no additional data to delete.

9. Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information. Since no personal data is transmitted to external servers, the primary security responsibility lies with Google's own services (AdMob, Sign-In, Play Billing), all of which use industry-standard encryption.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
